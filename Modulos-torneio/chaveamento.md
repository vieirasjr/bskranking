# 🏀 Tournament Match Formats --- Basketball (1x1, 3x3, 5x5)

## Overview

This document defines all supported competition formats for basketball
tournaments across modalities: - 1x1 - 3x3 - 5x5

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Supported Formats

-   Round Robin (Todos contra todos)
-   Knockout (Mata-mata)
-   Double Elimination
-   Group Stage (Fase de grupos)
-   Cross Groups (Grupos cruzados)
-   Home & Away (Ida e volta)
-   Swiss System

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 1. Round Robin

Each team plays all others.

Best for: small tournaments

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Knockout

Single elimination.

Best for: fast tournaments

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Double Elimination

Two losses required to eliminate.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Group Stage

Teams divided into groups, then advance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Cross Groups

Groups face each other in next stage.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Home & Away

Each match played twice.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 7. Swiss System

Matchmaking based on performance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Recommendation

1x1: - Round Robin - Swiss - Knockout

3x3: - Groups + Knockout - Double Elimination

5x5: - Groups + Knockout - Home & Away

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Notes

-   Support random draw
-   Support seeding
-   Handle BYE
-   Auto progression required
